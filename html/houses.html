House Stark

Rulers of the North from Winterfell, the Starks embody honor, loyalty, and resilience. Their motto, "Winter is Coming," warns of inevitable hardships. They descend from the First Men and are protectors of the realm, deeply tied to their ancestral godswood and direwolves.

House Lannister

Known for wealth, power, and intrigue, the Lannisters rule from Casterly Rock. Their motto, "Hear Me Roar," underscores their strength, but "A Lannister always pays his debts" defines their reputation. They use cunning and influence to maintain dominance, often at the cost of loyalty.

House Targaryen

The dragonlords of old Valyria, Targaryens ruled Westeros for centuries. Their motto, "Fire and Blood," reflects their fiery legacy. Driven by destiny and power, they often walk a fine line between greatness and madness, bonded to dragons that cement their mythic status.

House Baratheon

Fierce and headstrong, Baratheons hail from Storm's End, their motto "Ours is the Fury" mirroring their tempestuous nature. They were central to Robert’s Rebellion, toppling the Targaryens. Their strength lies in battle and family ties, though internal conflict often threatens their stability.

House Greyjoy

Fierce and headstrong, Baratheons hail from Storm's End, their motto "Ours is the Fury" mirroring their tempestuous nature. They were central to Robert’s Rebellion, toppling the Targaryens. Their strength lies in battle and family ties, though internal conflict often threatens their stability.

House Tyrell

Masters of strategy and wealth, the Tyrells rule the Reach, Westeros’s breadbasket. Their motto, "Growing Strong," symbolizes their flourishing influence. Known for their charm and cunning, they excel in politics, using alliances and subtlety to achieve power without resorting to open conflict.

House Martell

House Martell The Dornish rulers of Sunspear, the Martells embrace passion, pride, and vengeance. Their motto, "Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken," reflects their resilience. Fiercely independent, they resist traditional Westerosi norms, valuing freedom and equality, with a fiery culture that sets them apart from other houses.
