Season 1

Follow the noble houses of Westeros as they vie for control of the Iron Throne. This season introduces us to key players and sets the stage for the battles and betrayals to come.

Season 2

The War of the Five Kings erupts, with new alliances and rivalries forming. The battle for power intensifies, bringing both victories and devastating losses.

Season 3

As conflicts grow, shocking twists leave lasting impacts. This season includes the infamous Red Wedding, changing the landscape of the power struggle forever.

Season 4

The political tension reaches a peak with the arrival of new foes. Power shifts dramatically as events like the Purple Wedding unfold.

Season 5

The power struggle continues, with major characters facing moral dilemmas and severe consequences. The season ends on a cliffhanger that leaves fans in shock.

Season 6

Major players make a stand as old rivalries resurface. This season includes the Battle of the Bastards, one of the most visually stunning and epic battles in the series.

Season 7

Winter has finally come to Westeros, bringing icy threats and new alliances. The battle against the White Walkers is set in motion.

Season 8

The final season concludes the epic saga with climactic battles and decisions that shape the fate of Westeros forever. While divisive, it offers closure to a long and complex story.
