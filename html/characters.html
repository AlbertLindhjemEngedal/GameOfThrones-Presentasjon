Jon Snow

He joined the Night's Watch, where he proved himself to be a natural leader and formidable fighter. Secretly, Jon is the son of Lyanna Stark and Rhaegar Targaryen, making him the legitimate heir to the Iron Throne, a twist that changes the power dynamics in Westeros. His loyalty, sense of justice, and leadership were tested through numerous challenges, including battles against the White Walkers and his complicated relationship with Daenerys Targaryen.
